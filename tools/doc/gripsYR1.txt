gripsYR1              package:RCTRecruit               R Documentation

Daily recruitment data for the 1st year of the GRIPS study

Description:

     Modified recruitment data from the GRIPS study for the first year
     the study was conducted. The data includes the number of
     participants recruited each for each calendar date the recruitment
     was active.

Usage:

     gripsYR1
     
Format:

     A data frame with *159* observations of *2* variables

       [,1]  ScreenDt  ‘character’  Calendar date of the screening process for recruitment in the study 
       [,2]  Enrolled  ‘integer’    Number of new subjects enrolled in the study on that date           
      
Source:

     Villasante-Tezanos A, Kuo Y, Kurinec C, Li Y, Yu X (2024). "A
     non-parametric approach to predict the recruitment for randomized
     clinical trials: an example in elderly inpatient settings." _BMC
     medical research methodology_, _24_, 189. ISSN 1471-2288,
     <https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39210285/>.
